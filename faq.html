Frecuently asked questions

How do I log in?

You login with your Facebook account. You will either be requested to login or you can go to the widget in the top right corner and select login.

Why does it take a long time to load menus?

Our system is designed to pull in local menus whenever a user opens the application. If you are the first person in a new area, the loading may take a little longer than normal. The next time you use the app in the same area it will load more quickly.

How do I post a comment or take a Picture?

After you have rated an item, you will be given the option to leave a comment. The widget in the top right corner which will provide you with the option to upload an image and take a picture.

Can I post my recommendations onto other social media sites?

We will be integrating other social sites and community tools for our Wadjers over time. Tell us where you want your reviews, we need to know wadju think.

Can I change my recommendation once I’ve rated something?

Yes. You can always go back to an item and change the recommendation that you submitted for it. However, you can only have one rating registered at a time per item.

What do I do if the restaurant I selected doesn’t have a menu?

When you select a restaurant with menu, two options will appear: “Show Restaurant” and “Check again”. This request also triggers a notification in our office. Usually, if you press “Check Again”, the menu will load. Whenever we see a notification of a missing menu, we work on getting the it on the system. Some menus are not available online (believe it or not), which means it takes a little longer to get the menu into our system.

What do I do if the restaurant I’m looking for is not listed?

We are building the most up to date, cohesive list of restaurants available. If you have found a restaurant that we do not list, please email us at contact@wadju.com and we will hunt down that restaurant and get its menu for you.
